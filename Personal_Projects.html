Forggith Pharmaceuticals: Sales & Marketing Analysis

Forggith Pharmaceuticals is a pharmaceutical manufacturing company based in Germany. They produce various medical drugs which they get to consumers through their distributors. This project aimed to provide stakeholders with insights of their distributors and sales team using achieved sales metrics and the target metrics based on a four year period (2022 - 2025). The details of this task are published in a Medium article.

Quantifiable results include:
1. Sales quota attainment is at 130%.
2. Average stakeholder decision making progress increased by 15%.
3. 75% of users reported making more informed financial decisions.

Tools Used: Power Query and Tableau

Global Electronics Retailer: Data Cleaning and Exploratory Analysis

Global Electronics is a local retail store based in the United States that specializes in sales of all electronic products.
In this task, I developed an ETL process for cleaning the data and carrying out an Exploratory analysis on SQL, and used Power BI for visualization. This was done to remove discrepancies in the data and to provide detailed insights into business requirements.
Further detail into the task is available in my Medium article.

Key Insights uncovered are:
1. Top performing category were the computer sales with $18.6 million in total revenue, accounting for 34.6% of the total revenue.
2. Most purchases were customers who were residents of United States, having 53.7% of the total orders.
3. 79.3% of the revenue was generated from in-store sales.
4. North America was the region with the highest demand for electronics with over 123,000 orders.

Tools Used: SQL and Power BI

Northwind Sales: Data Extraction

The Northwind database is a sample database used by Microsoft to demonstrate the features of some of its products, including SQL Server and Microsoft Access. The aim of this project was to create a centralized system for data management, implement and ETL process for streamlining the data collection and analysis process (Data Cleaning and Extraction), and visualize the outcome (Business Requirements) using Tableau. Full detail of the task is in this Medium article.

The implementation of the automated ETL process significantly improved the efficiency and accuracy of Northwind's data analysis. Data retrieval times were reduced by approximately 40%, and data accuracy increased by approximately 30%. As a result, the company saw a 20% increase in sales forecasting accuracy, leading to improved inventory management and reduced stockouts.

Tools Used: SQL and Tableau

Superstore: Sales & Transactions Overview

Superstore is a retail company that needed an in-depth analysis of their sales performance to identify areas of strength and weakness, and make data-driven decisions to improve their sales processes. The result of this project was a comprehensive and interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a holistic view of the Superstore's sales performance. Go to Dashboard.

The Analysis revealed that:
1. Superstore's sales are strongest in the East and West regions of the United States.
2. Technology is the most profitable product category.
3. Standard Class is the most preferred ship mode with the highest revenue at $284,558.85.
4. October - December was the most profitable season, sales drastically increased during this period.

Tools Used: Power Query and Power BI

World Bank: Economic Indicators Analysis

The World Bank funds infrastructure projects in the developing world. As part of its mission, it releases an annual report on economic indicators. The task was to identify countries that have experienced the highest growth in population and GDP, and to understand the trends and factors influencing these indicators such as income, HDI and Life expectancy. Go to Dashboard.

The analysis also showed potential areas for improvement and growth.The following conclusions were gathered:
1. The average life expectancy grew by 12% from 1960 to 2018.
2. GDP per capita increased by approximately 184% globally from 1960 to 2018.
3. The Human Development Index saw an average increase of 22% from 1990 to 2021.

Tools Used: Excel and Tableau
