Global Electronics Retailer: Data Cleaning and Exploratory Analysis

Global Electronics is a local retail store based in the United States that specializes in sales of all electronic products.
In this task, I developed an ETL process for cleaning the data and carrying out an Exploratory analysis on SQL, and used Power BI for visualization. Go to Dashboard. This was done to remove discrepancies in the data and to provide detailed insights into business requirements.
Further detail into the task is available in my Medium article and GitHub.

Key Insights uncovered are:
1. Top performing category were the computer sales with $18.6 million in total revenue, accounting for 34.6% of the total revenue.
2. Most purchases were customers who were residents of United States, having 53.7% of the total orders.
3. 79.3% of the revenue was generated from in-store sales.
4. North America was the region with the highest demand for electronics with over 123,000 orders.

Tools Used: SQL and Power BI

Tracing Carbon Footprints: Data Exploratory Analysis of Global CO2 Emissions Over Time

The primary objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) on global CO2 emissions from 1750 to 2021, using a detailed dataset that includes fossil fuel CO2 emissions by country, paired with population data for the same years and countries. Further details of this is available in this Medium Article.
Link to Power BI Dashboard.

The analysis of global CO2 emissions using Power BI yielded several detailed insights and outcomes, some of which are:
1. United States and China are the top CO2 emitters, with China surpassing the United States in recent decades.
2. Countries with smaller populations (e.g., Saint Maarten - Dutch Part) have high per capita emissions, while populous countries like United States have lower per capita emissions.
3. Coal was the predominant fossil fuel contributing to CO2 emissions historically.

Tools Used: Power Query and Power BI

Analysing Aggregated Metrics to Generate Insights for a Digital Marketing Agency using SQL

Based on the analysis done above, the following insights were generated:
1. Interests with high composition values represent those that were most popular or relevant in that given month.

3. Rolling averages with steady increase, indicate growing interest and potentially emerging markets.
